Exhibitions

Leonardo da Vinci: The Exhibition

his exhibition brought together over 200 works by Leonardo da Vinci, including paintings, drawings, and manuscripts. It was the most comprehensive exhibition of da Vinci's work ever staged.

Tutankhamun: The Golden King and the Amarna Revolution

This exhibition featured over 150 artifacts from the tomb of Tutankhamun, the young Egyptian pharaoh who ruled in the 14th century BCE.

Picasso and Braque: The Cubist Encounter

This exhibition explored the artistic collaboration between Pablo Picasso and Georges Braque, two of the most important figures in the development of Cubism.
